## Getting started with Filebeat
Filebeat is another offering by Elastic that helps by offering a lightweight way to forward and centralize logs and files.

### 1. Dowload and Install Filebeat
Depending on your setup, proceed to the Elastic's [Official site](https://www.elastic.co/downloads/beats/filebeat) to download & install Filebeat.
Assuming you're installing on Ubuntu, you can follow [this guide](https://www.elastic.co/docs/reference/beats/filebeat/setup-repositories) to get started.
